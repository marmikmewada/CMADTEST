Meetings & Agendas

Meetings of the Cache MAD Board of Trustees are held the fourth Thursday of the month from January through October, then change to the second Thursday of November and December. All meetings begin at 7:00 pm. Meetings are held in the Bear River Environmental Health conference room (85 E 1800, North Logan). The agenda is posted here one week before the meeting; it is also available at the Public Meeting Notice website.
